# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in Solar a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on ideas and implementation details, not people.
- Give and receive feedback in good faith.
- Assume positive intent and ask clarifying questions before escalating.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments.
- Publishing private information without explicit consent.
- Repeated off-topic disruption in issues or pull requests.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, and pull requests that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately through the channel defined in `SUPPORT.md`.

All reports will be reviewed and handled with reasonable confidentiality.
